Braised Pork

Description

Red braised pork belly or hong shao rou is a classic pork dish from mainland China, red-cooked using pork belly and a combination of ginger, garlic, aromatic spices, chilies, sugar, star anise, light and dark soy sauce, and rice wine.

    Ingredients
  • Pork belly
  • Vegetable
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Scallion
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Star anise
    Steps
  1. blanch the pork belly
  2. Prepare the oil and sugar mixture to caramelize the pork
  3. The seasonings required for the braised pork
  4. Simmer the pork until tender
